About Tech Studio

CPCG Tech Studio is our internal innovation sandbox. It serves as an incubator for demonstrating practical technology solutions inspired by real operational challenges. These are not mass-market products—they are working demonstrations designed to show how automation, document intelligence, integrations, and analytics can enhance operational efficiency.

When a solution aligns with client needs, it can be delivered through:

  • API Subscription for internal system integration.
  • Custom Deployment tailored to client workflow, security, and infrastructure requirements.
